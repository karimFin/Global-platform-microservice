# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to a respectful, inclusive, and professional community.

## Expected behavior

- be respectful and constructive
- assume positive intent
- provide actionable feedback
- focus on ideas, not individuals

## Unacceptable behavior

- harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- abusive language or intimidation
- deliberate misinformation or impersonation

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close discussions, or restrict participation when behavior violates this policy.

## Reporting

Report concerns through private maintainer contact channels listed in `SUPPORT.md`.
