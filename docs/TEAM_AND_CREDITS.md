## Current contributor model

- This repository showcases real contributors and clearly marked open roles.
- No fake profiles or impersonation are used.

## Open contributor roles

- Platform Engineer: Kubernetes and deployment automation
- SRE Engineer: observability, alerting, reliability reviews
- Frontend Engineer: UX and performance improvements
- Data/Streaming Engineer: Kafka, CDC, and connector hardening

## How to join

1. Review `CONTRIBUTING.md`
2. Pick an issue with `good first issue` or `help wanted`
3. Open a PR to `dev`
